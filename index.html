Who I Am &
What I Build

I'm an AI and ML engineering student in the AI.SPIRE program by LevelUp Applied AI — a hands-on program focused on building production-ready AI systems from the ground up.

My journey started with setting up data pipelines and writing SQL queries, and has progressed through machine learning, deep learning with PyTorch, and building interactive analytics dashboards.

I enjoy the full stack of data work: cleaning messy data, engineering features, training models, and visualizing results. My goal is to build AI systems that solve real problems — not just run in notebooks.

I'm currently in Phase 4 (Analytics & Visualization) and working toward deploying full ML pipelines. By the end of the program, I'll have built 12+ production-style projects across the entire AI/ML lifecycle.
